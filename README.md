# Credit Card Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

---

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing credit card customer demographics and transaction behavior using Power BI. Two interactive dashboards were developed to provide business insights into customer segmentation, spending behavior, and revenue trends.

The dashboards help identify high-value customer groups, analyze transaction performance, and monitor weekly revenue growth for better decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics such as age, income, gender, and occupation
- Understand transaction trends and revenue patterns
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Track weekly revenue performance and growth
- Build interactive dashboards for business analysis

---

## Datasets Used

### Customer Data
Contains customer demographic information including:
- Age
- Gender
- Income
- Occupation
- Customer category details

### Transaction Data
Contains transaction-related information including:
- Revenue
- Card category
- Weekly transaction details
- Transaction counts

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Power Query
- Data Modeling

## Key Insights

- Female customers contributed higher revenue compared to male customers
- Medium income customers formed the largest customer segment
- Self-employed customers generated the highest revenue
- Blue card category contributed nearly 90% of total revenue
- Weekly revenue showed a positive growth trend of 10.15%
